LOUISE | THE VOICE

Every rocking band needs a voice capable of cutting through the mix with both soul and grit. Louise provides the soaring melodies and infectious energy that keeps the dance floor packed.

Louise serves as the magnetic vocal lead for Chord-ially Invited, anchoring the band with a rare blend of emotional depth and technical precision. Whether she's navigating the haunting, fragile dynamics of Radiohead's 'Creep' or unleashing the raw, defiant grit of The Cranberrie's 'Zombie', Louise delivers a vocal masterclass in every track. For her, the performance is about more than just the notes; it's about that shared, electrifying connection with the crowd. With a voice that commands the stage, Louise is the engine that keeps our dance floors packed and the energy high from the first chord to the final encore.
